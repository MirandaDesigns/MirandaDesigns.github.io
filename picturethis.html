Picture This App

Picture this is an app that was designed to allow nonprofessional photographers and photographers to capture great moments and make it more interesting and enjoyable when doing so. Picture this, is designed to have your favorite social media apps all in one. You can share locations, have a feed, tag people and discover new photographic experiences close to you.
